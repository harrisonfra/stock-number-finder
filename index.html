📦 Stock Finder

No photos yet

  1. Tap 📷 Add photos and pick your inventory photos (or take new ones).
  2. The app finds the sticky-note labels in each photo and marks them 🏷 to label. Open the photo and tap each note once to type its stock number. It also tries to read any printed text automatically.
  3. When something sells, type its number in the search box — the photo opens with the box's location highlighted. You can then mark it sold.

Missed a spot? Tap ＋ Tag in the photo and tap anywhere to add a number there.

🔒 Photos and stock numbers are stored only in this browser, on this device — nothing is uploaded anywhere. The first scan needs internet once, to download the free text-reading engine (~5 MB). Tip: use your browser's Add to Home Screen so it works like an app and your data is kept safe long-term.
